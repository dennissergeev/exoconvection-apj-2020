Overview of the Trappist-1e case

Interactive zoom and rotatable 3D visualisation (it may take a few minutes to load)

The MassFlux simulation covers the whole sphere, while the HighRes simulation is shown by superimposing a high-resolution grid that covers only the substellar region. The graphic shows cloud condensate (white isosurfaces), surface temperature (shading), and free troposphere wind vectors (arrows). The cloud condensate is shown using a threshold of total cloud condensate (liquid water plus ice).
